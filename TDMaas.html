Application Delivery Management as a Service (ADMaas)

Threat Detection Management as a Service (TDMaaS) is a cloud-based cybersecurity service that focuses on identifying and managing security threats within an organization's IT environment. TDMaaS solutions use advanced security technologies, threat intelligence, and expert analysis to detect and respond to security incidents and vulnerabilities proactively. This service is essential for organizations looking to strengthen their cybersecurity posture and protect against a wide range of threats, including malware, phishing attacks, and advanced persistent threats.

Security Information and Event Management (SIEM): Centralized collection, analysis, and correlation of security-related data from various sources to detect and respond to security threats.

Log Analysis: Monitoring and analyzing logs from network devices, servers, applications, and endpoints to identify anomalous activities and security incidents.

Intrusion Detection and Prevention Systems (IDPS): Real-time monitoring of network traffic to detect and respond to suspicious or malicious activities.

User and Entity Behavior Analytics (UEBA): Analyzing user and entity behavior patterns to detect abnormal or unauthorized activities.

Threat Intelligence Integration: Incorporating threat intelligence feeds and databases to stay up to date with the latest security threat information.

Incident Detection and Response: Identifying and responding to security incidents, including incident triage, investigation, and containment.

Vulnerability Assessment: Scanning and identifying vulnerabilities in the organization's IT environment, along with recommendations for remediation.

Threat Hunting: Proactive searching for signs of hidden or advanced threats within the network.

Malware Analysis and Detection: Identifying and mitigating malware, including zero-day threats.

Phishing Detection: Detecting and responding to phishing attacks targeting the organization.

Forensic Analysis: Investigating security incidents to understand their scope, impact, and root causes.

Compliance Management: Ensuring that the organization's security practices align with regulatory requirements and industry standards.

Benefits of Threat Detection Management as a Service include:

Proactive Threat Detection: Early detection of security threats and vulnerabilities, reducing the likelihood of successful attacks.

Timely Incident Response: Immediate response to security incidents, minimizing the impact and damage.

Expertise: Access to cybersecurity experts and advanced threat detection technologies.

Cost Efficiency: Lower total cost of ownership compared to maintaining on-premises security infrastructure.

Scalability: The ability to scale threat detection resources based on the organization's security needs.
